About Us

Our Mission

Oro Vision Care is an optometrist clinic proudly serving the community of Nagcarlan, Laguna. Our mission is simple: to help every patient experience clearer, healthier vision through compassionate and dependable eye care.

Our Values

We believe in creating a welcoming environment where you feel at ease from the moment you walk in. Whether you're here for a routine eye exam, a new pair of glasses, or specialized vision care, our team takes the time to understand your needs and guide you toward the best solutions.

Our Goal

At Oro Vision Care, your sight is our priority. We're dedicated to providing high-quality service, personalized recommendations, and eye care you can trust—so you can continue to see the world with comfort and confidence.

Our Services

Binocular Vision Assessment

A binocular vision assessment checks how well your eyes work together. This exam helps identify issues with eye alignment, depth perception, and coordination that may cause headaches, eye strain, or difficulty focusing. It ensures both eyes work as a team for comfortable and efficient vision.

Internal & External Eye Assessment

This comprehensive assessment examines both the inside and outside structures of your eyes. We check for signs of infections, dryness, allergies, cataracts, and other eye health conditions. It's an essential exam for maintaining healthy eyes and detecting issues early.

Pediatric Vision Assessment

Our pediatric vision assessment is designed specifically for children. It evaluates eye health, visual development, and early signs of vision problems that may affect learning and daily activities. We make the experience friendly and comfortable to help your child see their world clearly and confidently.

Low Vision Assessment

A low vision assessment helps patients with reduced vision that cannot be fully corrected with glasses or contact lenses. We evaluate your remaining visual abilities and recommend tools, strategies, and aids to help you maximize your sight and maintain your independence in daily life.
